# Acceptance Criteria — ACMP

This register reshapes ACMP's 66 acceptance criteria into the Keystone v1.0.0 table form so every `AC-###` is a defined, validator-recognized row. Each criterion is written as a Given / When / Then scenario, kept faithful to `docs/validation/acceptance-criteria.md`. The **Verifies** column carries the FR- and US- identifiers each criterion validates (Keystone gate G-TRACE), and **Phase** places each criterion in the delivery roadmap. All criteria here are PH-1 governance gates; they are immutable after approval and are superseded, never edited.

## Traceability

- Every Must-priority FR in `docs/requirements/functional.md` is covered by at least one AC (Keystone gate G-TRACE).
- AC-### to US-### and AC-### to FR-### mappings are carried in the **Verifies** column of each row.
- Governance rule coverage: authorization (AC-005 to AC-011), Segregation of Duties (AC-012 to AC-016, plus AC-007 for SoD-5), audit immutability and hash-chain (AC-017 to AC-020), voting integrity (AC-021 to AC-026), decision immutability and supersession (AC-027 to AC-029), topic lifecycle guards (AC-030 to AC-035), MoM versioning (AC-036 to AC-038), EN/AR and RTL (AC-039 to AC-042), accessibility (AC-043 to AC-046), unsaved-work guard (AC-047 to AC-048), file upload validation (AC-049 to AC-050), notification delivery (AC-051 to AC-053), background jobs (AC-054 to AC-056), aging (AC-057), membership (AC-058 to AC-059), search and traceability (AC-060 to AC-063), and dashboards (AC-064 to AC-066).
- All criteria are PH-1 gates; PH-1 exit requires all Must-priority ACs passed (per `docs/planning/roadmap.md`). Test evidence per AC is tracked in `docs/validation/acceptance-audit.md` and the TEST- register in `docs/validation/test-strategy.md`.
