Component library

The canonical building blocks for ACMP. Every token and component is defined once here and consumed by all screens. Each is built on design tokens, ships with light and dark themes, mirrors fully in Arabic (RTL), and meets WCAG 2.2 AA — visible focus, AA contrast, labelled controls, and ≥36px targets. Use the toggles above to inspect any state.

05

Buttons

One primary action per view. Secondary and ghost for supporting actions; danger only for destructive, confirmed actions.

Do · keep one primary per view; label by the action verb (“Publish agenda”).
Don't · stack multiple filled primaries or use danger styling for routine saves.

Rich-text editor

For minutes, rationale, and notes. Bilingual with a per-block direction control; autosave with an unsaved-changes guard; locks to read-only once minutes are published.

Autosaved

The committee reviewed the Keycloak adoption proposal. Identity and Platform streams support standardization; concerns were raised about the dual-running window.

Do · keep formatting minimal (headings, bold, lists, links); autosave and show a clear locked state once published.
Don't · allow editing published, immutable minutes, or mix fonts/colors that break the audit record.
